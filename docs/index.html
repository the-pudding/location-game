About

Guess the location in the USA in five tries. After each guess a new image is revealed. The color of the tile shows how close your guess was:

  • red = under 100 miles
  • yellow = under 50 miles
  • green = under = 20 miles

Data

About 500,000 geo-tagged images from Wikimedia Commons. Each day, a randomly generated location is chosen along with five random photos from within 10 miles.

By Russell Samora. A new puzzle is released daily at midnight, GMT.
