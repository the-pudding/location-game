How to Play

Guess the location in the USA in five tries. After each guess a new photo is revealed. The color of the tile shows how close your guess was within:

  • 5 miles
  • 100 miles
  • 200 miles
  • 200+ miles

Data

~500,000 geo-tagged images from Wikimedia Commons. Each day, a random location is generated, along with five photos within five miles.

By Russell Samora. A new puzzle is released daily at midnight, GMT. If you like our work, support us on Patreon, or send us a tip!
